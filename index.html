Sobre Mí

Hello, I'am Renato Pajares. I’m a Video Game Programmer, Game Designer, and Sound Designer at Gold Titan, a video game studio in Peru. I'm passionate about creating unique gaming experiences, combining my technical skills with creative design. At Gold Titan, I have participated in various projects, always striving to innovate and improve.

Why do I like making video games??

I like it because it's one of my greatest passions. Clearly, playing video games and making them are completely different processes, but just as video games have helped me have fun and overcome tough moments, I want to give back that help to others through the games I develop. The feeling of someone enjoying something you created is truly unique.
